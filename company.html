What we do

CC Agent is one API to route, govern, and audit every call your agents make. You point your code at a single endpoint, and every request is authenticated, rate limited, budgeted, routed, metered, and logged before it reaches a model. The live catalog is Anthropic Claude on Amazon Bedrock, with Anthropic Direct for failover. The design is model-agnostic: every model, one API, so you are not rebuilding your control plane each time the frontier moves.

Why now

Agents are moving from demos into production. The moment they do, two things become real at once: the spend, which grows quietly across teams and keys, and the risk, where an agent call can touch sensitive data, leave a region, or run up a bill with no one watching. The model layer matured fast. The governance layer around it did not. That gap is what we are closing.

How we are different

We took the compliant path on purpose, because the shortcuts are exactly what get a vendor cut at audit. Procurement runs through your own Bedrock account, or is managed through official AWS channels, subject to AWS and Anthropic official authorization and per-model limits. Every call is transparent down to the real upstream vendor, model, region, and account, with token counts taken from the upstream response.

  • Compliant procurement: your Bedrock, or managed via official AWS channels
  • Per-call transparency on the real upstream and reconcilable token counts
  • KYC-bound access and in-region routing your policy controls
  • Local entity-to-entity contracting, in your language and time zone

Where we are

We are early and pre-revenue, working closely with a small group of design partners to harden the product against real regulatory and procurement requirements. We do not claim named customers, and we do not invent metrics or certifications we have not earned. What we can show is how the controls map to the questions your risk team will ask.
