Data residency

Traffic stays in the regions you allow.

Details

In-region routing keeps agent traffic inside the regions your policy allows, with no silent cross-border hops on any call.

You set the allowed regions per project. A request that would leave the boundary is blocked, not quietly rerouted, and the region that served each call is recorded on its usage event.

  • Allowed regions set per project
  • No silent cross-border hops
  • Region recorded on every call

KYC & screening

Access binds to a vetted entity.

Details

Production access binds to a KYC-cleared legal entity. There are no anonymous production keys.

Entities are screened on a recurring basis against sanctions and entity lists, so access reflects current standing, not a one-time check at signup.

  • Keys bound to a vetted legal entity
  • Recurring sanctions and entity-list screening
  • No anonymous production access

Reconcilable billing

Every charge replays to the source.

Details

Every charge replays from usage event to ledger entry, line by line, against the upstream invoice. The commercial terms live on Pricing; here it is a compliance property.

Token counts come from the upstream response and are split into input, output, and cache, so a disputed line resolves against the source of truth, not an estimate.

  • Usage event to ledger, fully traceable
  • Input, output, and cache split per call
  • Reconciled against the upstream invoice

Immutable audit

Decisions and usage, logged.

Details

Routing decisions, usage, and operations are logged and exportable for review and procurement.

The record is append-only and carries the real upstream model, region, and account for each call, so reviewers see what actually happened, not a summary.

  • Append-only, tamper-evident log
  • Per-call vendor, model, region, account
  • Exportable for audit and procurement

Credentials secured

Keys never touch the repo.

Details

Upstream credentials live only in a managed secrets vault, never in the repo or environment files.

Keys are referenced, not copied, and access is scoped and logged, so a credential never sits in plaintext where it can leak.

  • Stored in a managed secrets vault
  • Never in the repo or env files
  • Scoped and access-logged

Delivered locally

Contracts and support in Mandarin.

Details

Contracts, DPA, and SLA reporting are delivered in Mandarin, with support on your business hours.

Procurement, legal, and finance work in their own language and time zone, which the upstream providers do not offer directly.

  • Mandarin contracts, DPA, and SLA reports
  • Support in China business hours
  • Entity-to-entity agreements
