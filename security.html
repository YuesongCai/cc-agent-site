Credentials

Upstream credentials live only in a managed secrets vault, encrypted with KMS. They are never committed to the repo or written into environment files. Keys are referenced at runtime, not copied, and every access is scoped to the service that needs it and written to an access log. A credential never sits in plaintext where it can leak.

Data handling

In-region routing keeps traffic inside the regions your policy allows, with no silent cross-border hops on any call. PII guardrails and data classification run in the data path, not as an afterthought, so sensitive content is caught before it leaves your boundary. Prompts and responses are not used to train models.

  • In-region routing, no silent cross-border hops
  • PII guardrails and data classification in the data path
  • Prompts are not used for training

Audit

Every call produces an append-only, tamper-evident record. Each entry carries the real upstream vendor, model, region, and account for that call, so reviewers see what actually happened rather than a summary. The full log is exportable for review and procurement.

  • Append-only, tamper-evident records
  • Per-call vendor, model, region, and account
  • Exportable for audit and procurement

Access & identity

Production keys bind to a KYC-cleared legal entity. There are no anonymous production keys. Entities are screened on a recurring basis against sanctions and entity lists, so access reflects current standing rather than a one-time check at signup. The console supports SSO and role-based access control, so people see only what their role allows.

  • Keys bound to a KYC-cleared legal entity
  • Recurring sanctions and entity-list screening
  • SSO and RBAC for the console

Compliance posture

The platform is designed to support SOC 2 and GDPR-aligned practices. To be clear and honest about where we stand: these certifications are being pursued, not yet held. We will not claim a badge we do not have. If your review needs a specific control mapped, bring it and we will show how it lines up.
