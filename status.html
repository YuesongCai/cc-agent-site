Availability target

The availability target is 99.9% on a monthly basis. The architecture is built to carry it: in-region routing across Amazon Bedrock, with Anthropic Direct as failover, so a single upstream issue does not take the gateway down.

This page is illustrative while production telemetry is being wired up. We do not publish historical uptime numbers until they come from live measurement. The 99.9% figure is design intent, not a contractual commitment, and carries no service-credit promise until a signed SLA is in place. See Trust & compliance for how that is being approached.
