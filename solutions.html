Finance

Control the spend.

See cost by team and project, with budgets that stop overruns.

Details

Spend is broken out by team, project, and key, taken from token counts in the upstream response rather than estimates. Finance sees where the money goes before the invoice arrives, not after.

Monthly budget gates stop spend the moment a cap is breached, so a runaway agent never turns into a surprise bill. Every charge reconciles line by line against the AWS invoice.

Risk & compliance

Pass the audit.

Every call logged with the real model, region, and account.

Details

Every call records the real upstream vendor, model, region, and account, with no silent downgrade. When an auditor asks where a request was served, the answer is in the record, not in an engineer's memory.

Routing stays in the region your policy allows, with no quiet cross-border hops. KYC and sanctions screening sit in front of access, and the audit trail is immutable and exportable for review.

  • Per-call vendor, model, region, and account
  • KYC and sanctions screening
  • In-region routing, no silent cross-border hops
  • Immutable, exportable audit trail

Engineering

Ship in a day.

Point one endpoint at us; existing agents keep working.

Details

The gateway speaks the Anthropic Messages API and an OpenAI-compatible API, so most agents move over by changing a base URL and a key. No rewrite, no new SDK to learn.

Underneath, requests route across multi-region Bedrock with Anthropic Direct failover, and streaming is passed through unmodified. Keys, budgets, and policy all live in the console for the team to manage.

  • Anthropic Messages and OpenAI-compatible APIs
  • Multi-region Bedrock with Anthropic Direct failover
  • Streaming passed through unmodified
  • Keys, budgets, and policy in the console
