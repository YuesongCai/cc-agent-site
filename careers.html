What we value

The product sits in the path of every call an enterprise agent makes. That sets the bar. We hire people who would rather ship something they can stand behind than something that demos well.

  • Correctness over hype. The audit trail has to match what actually happened.
  • Compliance as a design premise, not a feature bolted on at the end.
  • Clear writing. If you cannot explain it plainly, it is not done.
  • Ownership. You take a problem end to end and see it through.

Open areas

We hire by area, not by a fixed list of requirements. If your strength maps to one of these, the specifics are a conversation.

  • Gateway and runtime. The data path that routes, governs, and meters every call, in Go.
  • Control plane. Policy, billing, and audit services, in TypeScript and NestJS.
  • Frontend. The console where teams set policy and read usage, in Next.js.
  • Compliance and go-to-market. Mapping the controls to what regulated buyers actually ask for.
