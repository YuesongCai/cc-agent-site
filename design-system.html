02 · Color

Warm paper,
one plum.

Aubergine, not terracotta, not green. A soft mauve-grey paper holds the page like a sheet of warm stock. Plum is the only accent, used for links, marks, and the moments that matter. Ink anchors everything.

04 · Iconography

One family,
one weight.

Phosphor, regular weight, on a 24px grid. A single icon family across the whole product. Never mix weights or families in one surface. Icons carry the accent on dark, ink on paper.

06 · Motion

Calm, and
motivated.

Motion is quiet here, on purpose. It signals reliability, not flash. Every animation earns its place: revealing content, drawing a route, acknowledging a press. All of it collapses under reduced motion.

Reveal
Content rises and fades as it enters the viewport. Storytelling, not decoration.
y:20 to 0 · opacity · 700ms · (.16,1,.3,1)
Standard
The default ease for hovers, toggles, and small state changes.
ease (.4,0,.2,1) · 200ms
Spring
A gentle overshoot for the hero diagram and key arrivals. Used sparingly.
spring · soft · once

prefers-reduced-motion: reduce → all motion off, content renders complete.
