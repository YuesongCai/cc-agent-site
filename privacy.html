How we handle data on this site and in the service.

Read the terms

Last updated June 2026

This page explains, in plain terms, what data CC Agent handles and why. It covers two things: the small amount of data this marketing site collects, and the data that passes through the service when your agents route calls. This is a description of our practices, not legal advice.

Information we collect

On this site we keep collection minimal. We use basic, aggregate analytics to understand page traffic and improve the content. We do not build advertising profiles and we do not sell visitor data.

If you submit the demo request form, we collect what you enter: your name, work email, company, and your message. You choose what to put in those fields.

How we use it

We use the form details to respond to your demo request and to follow up about the service. We do not sell this information, and we do not share it except with the providers we rely on to operate the business, under agreement and only as needed to do the work.

Service data

When your agents call the service, prompts and responses are routed to the model provider you have configured. The live catalog runs on Amazon Bedrock across the regions you allow, with Anthropic Direct as failover. Traffic stays in those regions, with no silent cross-border hops.

We do not use your prompts or responses to train models. Content is processed to serve the request, to produce the usage and audit record, and to operate billing. Each call records the real upstream model, region, and account so the trail is reconcilable.

  • Routed to the model provider you configure, in the regions you allow
  • Not used for model training
  • Retained per your configuration, with a tamper-evident audit record

Retention

Service data is retained for the period set in your configuration. Audit and usage records are kept so charges can be reconciled and reviews can be supported, and they are removed on the schedule your settings and any contract define. Demo form submissions are kept only as long as needed to handle your inquiry and reasonable follow-up.

Your choices

You can ask us to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you, including a demo submission. Email the address below and we will act on the request. Service data deletion follows your configured retention and the terms of your agreement.

Contact

This policy describes current practice and may be updated as the service evolves. It is provided for transparency and is not legal advice.
